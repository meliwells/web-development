Strawberries and Cream

This strawberries and cream recipe reminds me of the ones we get at our local Strawberry Days Festival. This dessert is a refreshing summer treat and only requires a few simple ingredients.

Ingredients List

  • 4 cups of strawberries
  • 1 cup of whipping cream or half and half
  • 1/2 cup of granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 tsp. of vanilla extract

Recipe Steps

  1. Wash strawberries and de-stem.
  2. Slice strawberries and sprinkle sugar over the top and let them sit for 5 minutes.
  3. Gently toss the strawberries.
  4. Add and mix the whipping cream or half and half and vanilla with the strawberries and chill in the fridge for at least 2 hours.
